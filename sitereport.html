Site Report

I have developed this website with a very simple wireframe. The navigation part of the website contains logo of the website in the left side and home, contact, portfolio, work experience, and site report in the right side.

In the home page there is a brief introduction of the creator and it also contain a profile picture.

In the contact page the is a form that the visitor can use to contact with the creator. The form includes name, email, subject and message.

In portfolio section there are multiple phots of the website that the creator can developed. Portfolio section demonstrates the skill and talent of the creator

In work experience page there are multiple projects that the creator has been in. the projects include a brief description of the project, creators part, tools used, and timeline give.

Copyright information, links to the creator's social media profiles, and a call to action to sign up for updates are included in the footer area. The website's logo, tagline, and creator information are also included in the footer.

While muted purple is a more subdued, sophisticated color that may provide a feeling of depth and sophistication to the design, pastel yellow is a soft and inviting color that creates a welcome ambiance.These colors contrast well with one another, which make the website both aesthetically pleasing and simple to read. The mix of the light yellow and dark purple strikes a beautiful balance, and it may also be used to draw attention to specific website features.

In creating this website, there were several difficulties. A difficulty in itself was creating a website with excellent style and design. The website's layout and color scheme have undergone several alterations. The page’s content creation created a hurdle as well. It was challenging to decide what to include and what to exclude. The responsiveness of the website was the biggest obstacle. Further programming and design efforts were needed to make the pages responsive.
